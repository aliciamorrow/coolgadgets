Quick Overview

Improve your riding safety and help see what’s ahead with an ultra-bright BX-550 bicycle headlight from Night Provision.
With more and more cyclists on the roadways, it’s as important to be able to see what’s coming up as it is for cars to see you on the road. That’s why you need a powerful, rechargeable bicycle headlight from Night Provision.

Tough, Bright and Weather Resistant

Designed with aircraft grade aluminum, our front bike light is made for the modern rider who enjoys cycling for fun, sport or routine exercise. Featuring 550 lumens of ultra-bright white light in four different operating modes, you can flash, strobe or shine far ahead so you can see obstacles before they’re right on top of you. More importantly, you can ensure drivers and other cyclists can see you even on the darkest nights. Our lithium-ion powered bicycle lights are sure to brighten up your path no matter where the road takes you.

Easy-Click Mount

We’ve made installing our front headlight even easier by clicking an easy-click mount. Simply click it onto your front handlebars, snap in the light and you’re off. It’s that easy. And when you’re done, take it off, recharge it, and get ready for another ride!
